▼ Metrics explained

TWRR — Time-Weighted Return. Eliminates the effect of deposit timing. The correct metric to compare against S&P 500 or NASDAQ.

ROI — Return on Investment. Total gain over capital deposited. Affected by deposit timing.

MWR / IRR — Money-Weighted Return. Penalizes depositing large amounts before a downturn. Reflects real-world impact.

MDD (Maximum Drawdown) — La mayor caída histórica desde un pico hasta el punto más bajo. Mide el riesgo máximo que ha sufrido tu cartera.

Annualized — Converts cumulative return to a yearly rate: (1+r)^(1/years)−1.

Alpha — Your TWRR minus the benchmark (SPY or QQQ). Positive = you are beating the market.
